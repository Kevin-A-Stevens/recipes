Chili recipe

This Chili is the best.

  • 2 pounds ground beef chuck
  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 3 (15 ounce) cans chili beans, drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can chili beans in spicy sauce
  • 2 (28 ounce) cans diced tomatoes with juice
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 large yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 2 green chile peppers, seeded and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon bacon bits
  • 4 cubes beef bouillon
  • Â¼ cup chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic

Steps

  1. Crumble the ground chuck and sausage into the hot pan
  2. Pour in the chili beans, spicy chili beans, diced tomatoes and tomato paste.
  3. Add the onion, celery, green and red bell peppers, chile peppers, bacon bits, bouillon,
  4. Season with chili powder, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, oregano, cumin, hot pepper sauce, basil, salt, pepper, cayenne, paprika, and sugar.
  5. cover and simmer over low heat for at least 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
