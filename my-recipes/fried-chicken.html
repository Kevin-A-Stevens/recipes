Fried Chicken recipe

This Fried Chicken is the best.

  • 8 pieces of chicken
  • 1/4 gallon of Buttermilk
  • Half bottle of Frank's Hot Sauce
  • 1/4 jar of Pickle Juice
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Paprika
  • Flower
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Mix garlic powder and onion powder. Coat all chicken generously
  2. In bowl, pour Buttermilk, Hot sauce, and pickle juice
  3. Place chicken in bowl and cover with mixture
  4. Place in Fridge for 24 hours
  5. Mix Flour, salt, and pepper in large zip lock
  6. Remove chicken from buttermilk mixture and coat generously with flour mixture
  7. Heat oil and paprika in pan on medium
  8. Once heated, place chicken in pan and let fry for about 8-10 minutes per side
  9. Heat Over on 425
  10. Place chicken on cooking sheet and place in oven for 5 minutes
  11. Remove from oven and enjoy
